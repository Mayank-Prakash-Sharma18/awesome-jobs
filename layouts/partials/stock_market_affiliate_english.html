Suggested Books and Tools for Trading


1. Suggested books for Intraday and Swing Trading.

Secrets of a Pivot Boss by Franklin O. Ochoa

Secrets For Profiting in Bull and Bear Markets by Stan Weinstein

Trade Like a Stock Market Wizard by Mark Minervini

How to Make Money in Intraday Trading by Ashwani Gujral

2. Suggested books for learning about Derivatives (Options and Futures).

Option, futures and other derivatives by John C. Hull and Sankarshan Basu

Bible of Options Strategies by Guy Cohen

3. Suggested books for Trading Psychology and Risk Management.

Liar's Poker by Michael Lewis

The Intelligent Investor by Benjamin Graham

4. Some more good stock market related books worth reading.

Market Wizards: Interviews with Top Traders by Jack D. Schwager

Trading in the Zone by Mark Douglas
